About Myself

So a little about myself. I'm a recent graduate of General Assemblys' WDI course and have an intense passion for Web Development. All my life I’ve been surrounded by friends and family working in education, an interest I also share, so I followed in their track and became a swimming teacher. During my spare time, I became interested in coding, using chrome dev tools to view the very code behind my favourite websites. When I saw an advert for General Assembly and their WDI course, it sparked my curiosity and I quickly noticed similarities between teaching and coding such as creating a basic code on the computer could be seen as similar as using the basic functions of the swimming strokes and then refactoring and refining a child’s ability to swim. Whilst enjoying my time as a swimming teacher, the coding industry excited me and I became engrossed in developing my problem solving and logical thinking skills through creating and customising websites. I am driven by challenges and the quickly evolving coding industry has captured my interest and this is why I joined this 3-month immersive to satisfy my desire to know more. I am excited to continue building and developing my coding knowledge further. I would love to use my teamwork skills to tackle big challenges/problems in a small/medium sized company.

Interests

Brewdog

I first found an interest in Brewdog in my late teens whilst on Holiday with an exgf and her family. Since that day when I first tried Punk IPA Brewdogs flagship beverage I have been hooked. Years later Brewdog crowdsourced for the third time raising 4.25 Million Pounds I was part of this process and am a proud Equity Punk, the term used for those of us who have invested in the Brewdog Brand.

Swimming

I learnt to swim at a young age taking lessons until I was 8 which is when I then joined my local swimming club and started to compete, my greatest achievement coming when I came third in the 50m Butterfly at County level. As I grew older I found I had less time for swimming, but in the past two years I have picked swimming back up making time for something I find enjoyable. Being a competive soul I joined Teddington Masters with the eventual hopes of competing for them at International level.

Cycling

One of my many interests that I have is to cycle, and over the years I have managed to complete many sportives including the Prundential Ride 100, a 100 mile cycle ride out of London and around the Surrey Hills. My love of cycling doesn't end on the road and I can be found off-road travelling the Summer Lightening Trail also found in the Surrey Hills.
